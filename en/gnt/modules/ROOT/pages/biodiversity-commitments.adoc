== Preparation

. Learn about the Convention on Biological Diversity (CBD)
.. Watch introduction video on the https://youtu.be/H0Bp5jo4xic[Post-2020 Global Biodiversity Framework^] (YouTube - 1:14 min)
.. Watch video on the https://youtu.be/hnufsBNtFbw?si=Tl1ohccbNLOJRYmw[Post-2020 Global Biodiversity Framework and Targets^] (YouTube - 8:35 min)
.. Read https://www.cbd.int/doc/publications/cbd-sustain-en.pdf[Sustaining life on Earth^] (PDF 500 KB)
.. Investigate the CBD in your country (worksheet)
... Does your country have a current NBSAP? 
... Is your country updating its NBSAP? 
... If so, what is the state of the process? Who is coordinating it? 
... Are you engaged in the process - if so, how? If not, how could you be involved? 

. Learn about the Global Biodiversity Framework Kunming-Montreal
.. Read the https://www.cbd.int/gbf/[Overview^] (webpage)
